Welcome to Koa Surf Camp

Fun and affordable surf camp

Koa surf camp, offers fun, unique, and affordable way to adventure. Plan your surfing trip with us and we will ensure to bring you the most unforgettable and memorable experience. For surfers who are looking for affordable adventures, and intimate private hideaway place to stay in the heart of Tamarindo, Costa Rica, Koa surfer camp is the answer.

A brand new surf camp as the first enjoyable surfing style is closely located to beaches, 400m to Tamarindo Beach, 7 km to Grande Beach, and Conchal Beach 12km. Endless and enchanting views from the top is a perfect spot for admiring beautiful panoramic nature either awaiting gorgeous sunset to come, deeply breathing fresh air in paddy field, and enjoying Costa Rican surroundings’ harmony.
