Welcome to Koa Surf Camp

Fun and affordable surf camp

We are a family oriented business focused on offering fun, unique, and affordable way to adventure. Our surf instructors are WSL (World Surf League) certified.

Plan your surfing trip with us and we will ensure to bring you the most unforgettable and memorable experience. For surfers who are looking for affordable adventures, and intimate private hideaway place to stay in the heart of Tamarindo, Costa Rica, Koa surfer camp is the answer
